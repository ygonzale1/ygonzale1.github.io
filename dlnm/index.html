Scientific Methodology (DLNM)
What is a Distributed Lag Non-linear Model (DLNM)?

DLNM is a statistical framework used to study delayed and non-linear effects of an exposure on a health outcome.

What this tool does
  • Detects a reasonable family based on the outcome type.
  • Removes constant / invalid confounders automatically.
  • Generates a 3D response surface plus cumulative and lag-specific plots.
  • Uses a conservative lag spline by default to reduce overfitting.
